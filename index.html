About the Game

Don’t let the cards fool you!
Mystic Duel: Heroes Realm is a one-of-a-kind card battler where strategy, timing, and psychological prediction are the keys to victory.
Duel against real players in the Mystic Arenas, where every card is shared and every move could be your last.

  • Control a shared deck with your opponent—pull, merge, or discard strategically

  • Assemble the strongest lineup of mystical Heroes

  • Outsmart your rivals in rapid-fire, high-stakes PvP battles

Whether you're a seasoned tactician or new to card duels, Mystic Duel offers a fresh twist on the genre.
