How to Play

  • Choose a Hero Card – Each round, pick from 3 shared cards: pull, merge, or discard.

  • Predict the Opponent – Read your rival’s strategy and make the right move.

  • Unleash Hero Abilities – Activate powerful effects and combos mid-battle.

  • Build Your Deck – Collect unique heroes and customize your strategy.

  • Climb the Ranks – Win matches to rise in the global PvP arena.
